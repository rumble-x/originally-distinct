Duane Gordon Jr.

Artist’s Statement

Duane Gordon Jr.’s auesthetic comes from the urban landscape and social injustices he’s experienced growing up in SW Philadelphia. He soon realized his passion for creativity when he started painting and design under th e name “Originally distinct”.



The direct questions and catchphrases he uses are an integral part of his imaginary and signature element to his lifestyle brand. He aims to inspire others to embrace their unique individual style and to light a path for emerging artists to become truly self-sufficent.



With his work on various mediums, you’ll be inspired bythe story that goes behind each project. Currently, he is preparing to launch “orignally distinct” gallery in Old City this summer 2021.
